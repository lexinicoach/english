About

Hi! I'm Lexi. I'm an online English teacher based in New Zealand. My speciality lies in accent reduction and culture adaptation. I've lived, studied and worked in the UK, TW and China during my early years. Now I've embarked on a new journey in the Kiwi land.

My coaching methods work exceptionally well with shy students and those who are taking the IELTS exams. With my clinical psychology background, I provide many tips and tricks to help you overcome your fear of speaking a foreign language. Helping you build skills and confidence for establishing rapport with native speakers.

If you need guidance to achieve confident, native sounding speech, talk to me now!
