About Me

Hi! I'm Lexi. I'm a professional online communication coach based in Taiwan and China. My speciality lies in accent reduction and culture adaptation. I've lived, studied and worked in the UK during my early years. My coaching methods work exceptionally well with IELTS speaking and writing.

With my psychology background, I provide many tips and tricks to help you overcome your fear of speaking a foreign language. Helping you build skills and confidence for establishing rapport with native speakers.

If you need guidance to achieve confident, native sounding speech, I am the one you're looking for!
